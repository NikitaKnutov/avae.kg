About AVAE Capital

AVAE Capital combines several types of professional activities on the securities market.

The company has a licence from the Financial Market Regulation and Supervision Service of the Kyrgyz Republic.

The license allows AVAE Capital to carry out a wide range of operations in the financial market, including brokerage activities, client asset management and investment consulting. The company is committed to providing its customers with high quality services that meet the highest industry standards.

Brokerage activities

AVAE Capital, being a licensed professional participant of the financial market, provides access to trade a wide range of financial instruments to different categories of clients (retail / professional client, market counterparty).

Trust management

AVAE Capital provides a service that allows clients to transfer their assets for management to generate income.

Dealership activities

AVAE Capital is the nexus for securities purchase and sale transactions.
